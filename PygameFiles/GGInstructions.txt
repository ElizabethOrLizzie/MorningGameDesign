Your neighbors are planning to go on a lovely vacation.
But how will they rest without anyone to look after their precious flowers?
They decide to bestow the title of garden guardian upon you.
Protect your neighbors flowers from malicious weeds while they are away.

Use the arrows to move
Run into weeds to attack them
Avoid running into flowers
Going to close to the trees will make you go back to the top left
Going to the leaderboard is necessary to save your score
